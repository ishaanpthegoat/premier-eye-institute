Contact Lens Exams & Fittings

Finding the contact lens that fits your life. Scroll to walk through your visit, step by step.

  1. Step 1 of 7

    It starts with your day

    Screens, sports, allergies, how often you'd wear them — we talk through your routine first, because that decides which lenses actually suit you.

    About 5–10 min ⏱ PLACEHOLDER

  2. Step 2 of 7

    Measured to fit

    We measure the curve of your eye and check your tear film, so your lenses sit comfortably and stay comfortable all day.

    About 10 min ⏱ PLACEHOLDER

  3. Step 3 of 7

    So many options

    Daily, extended-wear, disposable, gas-permeable — modern lenses come in many types. We'll explain the trade-offs and narrow it to the right one.

    About 5 min ⏱ PLACEHOLDER

  4. Step 4 of 7

    Try before you decide

    We place trial lenses on your eyes and give them a few minutes to settle, so we can see exactly how they feel and fit on you.

    About 15–20 min to settle ⏱ PLACEHOLDER

  5. Step 5 of 7

    Checking the fit

    We look at how the lens moves and centers on your eye, and fine-tune the choice until it's right.

    About 5–10 min ⏱ PLACEHOLDER

  6. Step 6 of 7

    New to contacts? We'll teach you

    If it's your first time, we'll walk you through putting lenses in, taking them out, and caring for them, until you feel confident.

    About 15–20 min ⏱ PLACEHOLDER

  7. Step 7 of 7

    Ordered and set

    We finalize your prescription, help you order, and set a follow-up to make sure everything's still comfortable.

    About 5 min ⏱ PLACEHOLDER

The whole visit

About 45–75 minutes, often paired with your eye exam. ⏱ PLACEHOLDER
