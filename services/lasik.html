LASIK Co-Management

LASIK, from your first question to your final follow-up. Scroll to walk through your visit, step by step.

  1. Step 1 of 5

    Start with a real conversation

    LASIK can correct nearsightedness, farsightedness, and astigmatism. First we check whether you're a good candidate, honestly and without pressure.

    First visit, about 30–45 min ⏱ PLACEHOLDER

  2. Step 2 of 5

    Precise measurements

    We map your eyes in detail and prepare everything your surgeon needs, so your procedure is planned around your exact eyes.

    About 30 min ⏱ PLACEHOLDER

  3. Step 3 of 5

    Matched with a trusted surgeon

    We work alongside experienced LASIK surgeons and coordinate your procedure, so you're in the right hands on surgery day.

    Scheduled with a partner surgeon ⏱ PLACEHOLDER

  4. Step 4 of 5

    The procedure is quick

    The LASIK procedure itself is fast and done at the surgical center. You'll rest, then head home the same day.

    The procedure is about 15 min ⏱ PLACEHOLDER

  5. Step 5 of 5

    We handle your recovery

    Your follow-up visits happen right here with us. We track your healing and make sure your vision settles in beautifully.

    Follow-ups over the days and weeks after ⏱ PLACEHOLDER

The whole visit

The full journey spans several weeks; most of your visits are short, and your care stays local. ⏱ PLACEHOLDER
