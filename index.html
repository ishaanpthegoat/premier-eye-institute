Optometrist & owner

Meet your doctor

Real experience — and time to listen.

Dr. Nisha Mehta opened Premier Eye Institute in 2014 because she wanted to do eye care her way: thorough, personal, and never rushed. She has an MBA from East Carolina University and has examined more than 5,000 eyes here in Creedmoor.

  • Pennsylvania College of Optometry, with a residency in eye disease at the Baltimore VA

  • Fellow of the American Academy of Optometry and former clinical assistant professor at UNC Chapel Hill

  • Volunteer eye care on mission trips to Guatemala
